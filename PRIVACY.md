## What I Collect

Nothing. AdapterForge is a local CLI that reads the files and paths you pass on the command line, calls `mlx_lm`, `ollama`, and a local llama.cpp checkout as subprocesses, and writes its output to the paths you specify. It makes no network requests, sends no telemetry, and has no analytics of any kind.

## Your Data

Any dataset, model weights, or adapters you point AdapterForge at stay on your machine, in the directories you choose. Nothing is uploaded anywhere by this tool.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the update will be committed to this file with a note in `CHANGELOG.md`.
